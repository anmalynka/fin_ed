> A lightweight, goal‑driven financial‑planning assistant that speaks the language of real people.

## 🎯 What It Does

* **Goal‑centric** – Focuses on the *specific* savings and investment objectives that matter to you.
* **Data‑driven** – Uses your income, expenses, burn rate, and current net‑worth snapshot to calculate the *impact* of every decision.
* **Scenario‑analysis** – “What if I take a 3‑month sabbatical?” → “This will push your retirement age back by X years.”
* **Actionable recommendations** – Simple, actionable steps you can implement immediately, no fancy trading knowledge required.
* **Designed with people in mind** – Particularly tailored for women, because that demographic often has fewer formal finance lessons – *not* because they’re less capable.  
  The language is friendly, the calculations are transparent, and every suggestion is backed by data.
